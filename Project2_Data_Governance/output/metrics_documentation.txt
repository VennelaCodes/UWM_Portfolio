METRIC: Operational Efficiency Index (OEI)
  Range: 0-100
  Formula: Composite score combining closure rate (40%), turnaround performance (40%),
   and escalation rate (20%), with rework penalty applied.
  Interpretation: Higher is better. Target: >= 60

METRIC: Client Experience Score (CES)
  Range: 0-100
  Formula: Weighted blend of CSAT (50%), NPS (30%), with complaint rate penalty.
  Interpretation: Higher is better. Target: >= 70

METRIC: Capacity Utilization Rate
  Range: 0-100%
  Formula: Ratio of applications reviewed to applications received.
  Interpretation: Higher is better. Target: >= 85%

METRIC: Process Health Score
  Range: 0-100
  Formula: Inverse measure of rework frequency and system downtime.
  Interpretation: Higher is better. Target: >= 75

METRIC: Team Productivity Index
  Range: ratio
  Formula: Loans processed per 100 client calls handled.
  Interpretation: Higher indicates more efficient client interactions.
